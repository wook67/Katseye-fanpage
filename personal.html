    Katseye is a global girl group based in Los Angeles. Formed through the 2023 survival series The Debut: Dream Academy. A collaboration between HYBE and Geffen Records. The aim of the group was to combine both the heavy training of Kpop idols, but also with a diverse background with many different cultures. The group officially debuted on June 28, 2024, and has since become a viral sensation, even earning a 2026 GRAMMY nomination for Best New Artist.
